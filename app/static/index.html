Why Kipaji Chetu

Built for the classroom.
Designed for every student.

Three capabilities that work together to close learning gaps before they become permanent.

01

AI Personalization

The system reads every answer in real-time. Struggling on fractions? The next question meets the student there — no teacher configuration required.

  • Difficulty adapts per session
  • Instant AI-generated feedback
  • Tracks knowledge gaps over time
  • Works across all 8 subjects
02

Accessibility Tools

Every question and every piece of feedback can be read aloud. Students with dyslexia, visual impairments, or low literacy gain full access to learning.

  • Text-to-speech on all content
  • Voice input for answers
  • Simplified explanation mode
  • High-contrast readable interface
03

Teacher Analytics

At a glance: who is falling behind, which topics need re-teaching, where the class average stands — no manual grading, no spreadsheets.

  • Struggling student priority list
  • Topic difficulty breakdown chart
  • Class average score card
  • Live data, refreshes on demand
Process

Four steps.
One classroom
transformed.

From login to live insight — the full loop takes under ten minutes the first time, and runs automatically after that.

Try It Now
1

Student

Secure Login

The student enters their school-issued ID. No passwords, no email addresses — one number and they are in. The system loads their performance history instantly and picks the right difficulty level.

Privacy-first, no personal data stored
2

Selection

Choose a Subject

A clean topic grid presents available subjects. The student picks one — Mathematics, Science, History, and more — and the AI generates a tailored first question calibrated to their recent performance.

8 subjects, expanding quarterly
3

Learning Loop

Answer & Understand

Multiple-choice options appear. The student selects, submits, and receives AI feedback that explains the answer in plain language. TTS reads everything aloud on demand. Wrong answer? The next question reinforces the same concept at a gentler level.

All content speakable in one click
4

Teacher

Monitor & Act

The teacher dashboard updates in real-time. Struggling students surface to the top. Topic difficulty charts show where the whole class needs extra instruction — without any manual data entry or grading.

Dashboard refreshes with one click
